Why We Built This
Artificial Intelligence in recruitment holds the promise of true meritocracy—but only if we actively prevent it from inheriting human biases. We built FairAI Studio to demonstrate that bias detection and mitigation shouldn't be an opaque process. By providing a transparent, interactive dashboard that visualizes the direct impact of fairness algorithms, we empower teams to build models that evaluate candidates purely on merit, eliminating demographic disadvantages.

📖 Metric Glossary

Disparate Impact (DI)
Ratio of selection rates between groups. Ideal = 1.0. Values below 0.8 indicate legal discrimination (4/5ths rule).
Statistical Parity Difference
Arithmetic difference in selection rates. Ideal = 0.0. Negative = privileged group selected more.
Equal Opportunity Difference
Difference in True Positive Rates. Ideal = 0.0. Measures if qualified candidates are treated equally.
Sensitive Feature
Gender (0 = Female / Unprivileged, 1 = Male / Privileged). Bias is measured across this dimension.
